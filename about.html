WELCOME TO ESCENTIALS

Founded in the bustling heart of Los Angeles in 2024, ESCENTIALS has been crafting an immersive world of scents for a decade. Our founder, a passionate perfumer with a vision for customizable luxury, transformed her small workshop into a beloved haven for fragrance enthusiasts.

Inspired by the vibrant and diverse tapestry of city life, we ventured into the realm of perfumery with a desire to bottle the indescribable. Our scent library is a curated collection of the world's finest natural essences and innovative molecules.

At ESCENTIALS, we understand that scent is deeply personal. That's why we offer an interactive customization experience. Through private consultations and guided sensory exploration, our expert perfumers work with you to create a fragrance that resonates with your spirit and lifestyle.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. Every ESCENTIALS product is meticulously formulated and responsibly sourced. We pride ourselves on our cruelty-free practices and sustainable sourcing, ensuring that the luxury you indulge in doesn't come at the expense of our planet.
